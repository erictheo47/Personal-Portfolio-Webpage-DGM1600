About The Cards.

The Pokemon franchise is great in the way that every Pokemon each has their own unique traits and abilities, which makes collecting them as trading cards that much more worth it.

Different Pokemon even have different affinities and tolerances to the elements.

Below, you'll find a strong collection of trading cards based on the popular Trading Card Game, Anime Series and Video Game Collection, Pokemon.

Add your favorite first generation Pokemon to the list and build the ultimate collection!
