About The Cards.

The Star Wars franchise is great in the way that every character each has their own unique traits and abilities, which makes collecting them as trading cards that much more worth it.

Rey Skywalker (Palpatine) can heal using the force, Jedi who have passed on, such as Master Yoda or Obi-Wan Kenobi, can reach out to others using the force to offer guidance. Different species even have different affinities and tolerances to the elements.

Below, you'll find a strong collection of trading cards based on the characters, creations and creatures from the first seven Star Wars films.

Add your favorite characters to the list and build the ultimate collection!
